Our Technical Societies

Explore our active society chapters, each focusing on a specific field of interest.

Computer Society (CS)

Focuses on the theory, design, and development of computer systems and networks. We host workshops on programming, cybersecurity, and software engineering.

Communication Society (ComSoc)

Dedicated to the advancement of communications technology. Our activities include seminars on 5G, wireless networks, and optical communications.

Women in Engineering (WIE)

An affinity group dedicated to promoting women engineers and scientists and inspiring girls to follow their academic interests in engineering.

SIGHT (Special Interest Group on Humanitarian Technology)

Leverages technology to solve real-world humanitarian problems. Our projects focus on sustainable development and community welfare.
