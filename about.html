About IEEE

A legacy of innovation and a future of excellence.

A Global Legacy

IEEE was founded in 1884, with iconic figures like Alexander Graham Bell and Thomas Edison among its charter members. Today, IEEE serves over 400,000 members worldwide, with more than 100,000 Student and Graduate Student members who are essential to its continued growth and vitality. Not only is IEEE the world’s largest technical and professional society, but it also publishes a significant amount of the world's literature in electrical engineering and computer science.

Our Student Branch at SMVITM

The IEEE membership is geographically divided into ten Regions. Our branch, the IEEE Student Branch of SMVITM (Code: STB10147), proudly falls under the Bangalore Section and the Mangalore Sub-Section within Region 10. We are dedicated to bringing the global mission of IEEE to our local campus community.

Our Mission

IEEE’s core purpose is to foster technological innovation and excellence for the benefit of humanity.

Our Vision

IEEE will be essential to the global technical community and to technical professionals everywhere, and be universally recognized for the contributions of technology and of technical professionals in improving global conditions.
